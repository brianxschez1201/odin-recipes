Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Scrambled eggs mixed with ham, vegetables, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 large brown eggs
  • 2 1/2 slices of ham
  • 1 green onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Avocado oil spray
  • Salt
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Slice ham into small pieces.
  2. Slice green onion into small pieces.
  3. Crack eggs onto a small bowl and stir.
  4. Add salt to the eggs to your liking.
  5. Spray avocado oil on a pan.
  6. Heat on a stove at high for about a minute.
  7. Toss spinach, ham, and onion on the stove.
  8. Stir around vegetables with a spatula until they start sizzling.
  9. Pour the egg onto the pan of vegetables.
  10. Stir/scramble until scrambled eggs are cooked to your liking.
